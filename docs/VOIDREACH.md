# Voidreach — Outer Sector Guide

**Starlane 1.0.31** — Hidden outer sector reachable only via a wormhole in Nyx. Home of the Vael aliens, alien bases, purchasable alien tech, and hard space weather (radiation, ion storms, EM blackout).

---

## How to find it

1. Jump to **Nyx**
2. Fly the **far southwest rim** (away from freelanes — roughly toward the system edge)
3. Approach the dim **spatial anomaly** until it charts as **Unstable Rift** (+750 cr discovery)
4. Press **F** to transit → **Voidreach**

**Return:** use the **Return Rift** near Voidreach spawn to jump back to Nyx.

---

## What’s there

| Content | Details |
| ------- | ------- |
| **Vael aliens** | Skimmers, **Stalkers**, & Wardens — hostile, unique art, faction-colored teal bolts, rich loot |
| **Spire of Vael** | Main alien base (Vael Collective); sells tech; Outfit list is scrollable |
| **Resonance Anchorage** | Second base; heavy defenses |
| **Alien corridors** | Freelane-style rings between spires / rift |
| **Chart bonus** | First visit +2000 cr + achievement **Beyond the Veil** |

Human freighters and pirate dens do not spawn here — Vael dominate open space.

## Alien tech (Outfit at Vael docks only)

| Tech | Cost | Effect |
| ---- | ---- | ------ |
| Vael Plasma Locus | 8500 cr | +35% laser (and other DE) damage |
| Void Shroud | 7800 cr | +30 max shield, +20% regen |
| Gravitic Drive | 7200 cr | +20% thrust & top speed |
| Crystal Lattice Hull | 8000 cr | +40 max hull |
| Phase Needle Array | 9000 cr | Faster fire rate (laser / plasma / pulse / rail) |
| Neural Tractor | 5500 cr | +80% salvage tractor range |

Open the **Outfit** tab at Spire of Vael or Resonance Anchorage — alien tech appears as **✦** rows after the base services list. Use **↑/↓** to scroll; Enter to purchase and install.

## Combat notes

- Vael fire **plasma** and **pulse** patterns (teal bolts) plus occasional missiles
- Wardens are tanky multi-shot; Stalkers are mid-weight hunters; Skimmers are fast
- Fit a hangar **Rail Lance** or **Plasma Repeater** (Ship Hangar), scale with **Gun Amplifiers Mk** and **Power Amplifiers Mk**
- Weather here often applies a light screen grade (visual); damage rates still show on the HUD strip

## Galaxy map & missions

- **Voidreach** and the purple dashed wormhole link stay **hidden** until the rift is discovered (or Voidreach is visited).
- Mission board never auto-routes cargo or escorts into Voidreach.
- Cartographer achievement still counts the **10 frontier systems** only; Voidreach is outer sector.

---

## Space weather

Voidreach often has harsh zones (radiation, ion, EM). Watch the top-center weather strip for live rates; a soft full-screen tint appears in hazards without hiding ships. Freelane corridors help cross the system safely.

---

## Lore notes

- Beyond charted space; the Vael built cities in the dark.
- Spire of Vael: alien basalt spire — “sells impossible tech.”
- Resonance Anchorage: harmonic docks — gravity feels wrong.
- Star: **Vael Light** (greenish). Nebula tint is deep teal/void green.
